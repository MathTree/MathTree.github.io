Differential Equations

This course studies specific equations (and their solutions) that relate functions to their derivatives. This becomes extremely useful in many fields of Engineering, Math, Physics and much more.

▸ Introduction to Basic Concepts - Basic things that need to be known
⁃ Definitions of Differential Equations - What is the definition and what are initial values?
⁃ Direction Fields - What they are and how to sketch them
▸ First Order Differential Equations - Observing what first order differential equations are and standard methods for solving them
⁃ Linear Equations, Separable Equations, Exact Equations, Bernoulli Equations, and their solution methods
⁃ Modeling Problems With First Order Differential Equations
⁃ Equilibrium Solutions
⁃ Eulers Method
▸ Second Order Differential Equations - Similar process as first order. What are they and what are the methods for solving them
⁃ Derive Characteristic Polynomial and use Principle of Superposition
⁃ Real, Complex, and Repeated Roots
⁃ Fundamental Sets of Solutions
⁃ The Wronskian
⁃ Undetermined Coefficients and Variation of Parameters
⁃ Series Solutions
▸ Higher Order Differential Equations - We have looked at first and second order equations, but what about n-order equations?
⁃ Basic ideas for solving n'th order differential equations
⁃ Solving Linear Homogeneous Differential Equations
⁃ Undetermined Coefficients and Variation of Parameters for n'th order equations
⁃ Laplace Transforms
⁃ Systems of Differential Equations

Relationship

▸ Calculus II
This course is pretty related to Calculus I and II. Derivatives and Integrals are fundamental and are used as tools to get solutions to these equations. Students should be comfortable in integration and differentiation skills.
