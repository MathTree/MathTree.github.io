Math Atlas

Enough about me. The reason I made this website was because during this entire 4 year spaghetti-esque sprawl I realized that learning is a TON easier if you actually know what the hell you're supposed to be learning. I often found in class that I was just learning random facts, which were sometimes cool, but otherwise useless without some goal. But, nearing the end of each course I would usually realize why they were teaching me what I was being taught. If I were to have understood why I was being taught the things I was in the first place, I feel I would have performed significantly better. So, I created this website to show the relationships between mathematical concepts and courses, to provide resources I found useful, and anything relevant to making learning math easier and more clear for not just undergraduates, but anyone learning the field.
